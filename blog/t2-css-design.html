The difference between Margin, Border, and Padding

April 1, 2015

The difference between Margin, Border, and Padding is often difficult to decipher. Many students get caught up in the subtleties. The following LINK helps to illustrate the differences between the elements.

It is helpful to start with the Content. Envision a square box. Your content is the innermost space. It is where you put the words of a blog entry for example.

The next element is the Padding. This is the space surrounding your content. It extends from the content to the edge of the square box.

The subsequent element is the Border. This is the actual lines forming your square box.

Finally, there is the Margin. This is the outermost space. This extends outside of your square box to the edge of the page. There is no other content on your website beyound the Margin.
