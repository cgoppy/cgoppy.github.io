Bio

Attorney Yifei He, the President of the Law Firm, is admitted to practice law before the State and Federal Bars of New York, Massachusetts, and Connecticut. Yifei obtained his Juris Doctorate from the University of Connecticut, where he served as Editor of the Connecticut Public Interest Law Journal. He is also an active member and chair of New York County Lawyers Association (NYCLA) and the New York City Bar Association (NYCBA).

Practice Areas

Business Incorporation

We follow a two step process in setting up your business: First, we will provide a consultation regarding the type of entity your business should form; taking into consideration your unique needs and goals. Second, we will represent you in registering your entity; minimizing cost and maximizing efficiency. Overall, we will provide a fast and friendly service; suitable for any type of business.

Intellectual Property Registration

We provide a soup to nuts service for registering your trademark and/or copyright with the United State Trademark and Patent Office. We are licensed professionals who have successfully handled countless brands on behalf of our customers. As such, we are completely familiar with the nuances of the registration process. Not only do we ensure that your registration is done 100% error free, we also ensure that your mark is in compliance with all relevant requirements.

Entertainment Law

We rely on both copyright and trademark law to protect the intellectual property rights of our clients. Commercial Litigation
